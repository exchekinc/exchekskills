# exchek-onboarding

Guided onboarding tour for your ExChek Engine. Walks through all 22 compliance skills with practical, industry-specific examples. Tracks your progress so you can resume across sessions.

## Usage

```
/exchek onboarding
```

## What it does

Walks you through 4 modules:

1. **Classify & Screen** — ECCN classification, jurisdiction, denied party screening, red flags, country risk
2. **License & Document** — License determination, export documentation, recordkeeping
3. **Risk & Compliance Programs** — Risk triage, ECP generation, compliance reports, audit lookback
4. **Engine Features** — CRM/ERP connectors, auto-updates, analytics dashboard

Examples are tailored to your industry and company profile from `.exchek/config.json`. Progress is saved between sessions.

## After onboarding

```
/exchek classify [item]   — Start classifying
/exchek screen [party]    — Screen denied parties
/exchek                   — Full dashboard
```
